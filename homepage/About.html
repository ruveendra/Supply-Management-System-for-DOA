About Aroma Villa

Aroma homestay is situated in a very calming and relaxing greenish environment with paddy fields around. You can enjoy the view of various endemic birds including peacocks while you are enjoying your breakfast tea. Aroma homestay is located nearby to the city and is situated in a distance of 5 minutes time from the southern highway. You can find supermarkets, banks, hospitals, pharmacies etc. nearby to the homestay. We have very kind and friendly staff to accommodate your needs at any time.
